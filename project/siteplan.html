Step 2 - Site Content

  1. Site Name: The SulejaHHMC Design Home Page was choosen for it focuses on small and medium scale loan fro artisans
  2. Site Purpose: Describe SulejaHHMC Design Works services and training. Offer free downloadable copies of Loan Application forms. These apllication form have been freely downloadable for 3+ years.
  3. Scenarios:
    • How can I download SulejaHHMC forms? (At the end of each form is a short description of the types of training offered by sulejaHHMC-design)
    • What types of training and serviices are offered by SulejaHHMC?
    • How can I contact sulejaHHMC-design to request information about credit facility and other services?
  4. Color Schema: The colors are defined in the :root element at the top of styles/sulejaHHMC.css file. I am currently using --sunburst_red: for the logo text and --back_color1: for the footer. More colors will be used as the project progresses
  5. Typography:
    I explicityl use Roboto family in both the body and h1-header
