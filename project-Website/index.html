Our Story

The Suleja Helping Hand Multipurpose Cooperative Limited was established in the year 2016. The cooperative are solely based on providing facilities to small and medium scale businesses and other trnsaction. Following are the aims of the cooperative
  • Establishing the cooperative in all 36 states and Federal Capital Territory Abuja of Nigeria in this first year of operation through partnership with relevant organizations with headquarters located in Abuja.
  • Generating funds through our thrift /savings of at least Ten million naira (N10, 000,000.00) within the first year of operation (2016), through individual contributions and profit from investment.
  • Establishing at least one Suleja Ward Microfinance Bank(s) in all major cities/ recommended towns of all states and Abuja of Nigeria within two years of operation (2016-2018).

Requirements for facility/Guarantors

  • ⁘All Loans and Business transactions must have guarantors who are members of the cooperative and these guarantors would be fully responsibile in case of default.
  • ⁘ Guarantors shall not be allowed to take substantial loan untill and after the person they stool surety for has paid back at least twenty percent(20%) of the amount collected.
  • ⁘To qualify for loan, the intending borrower must present a guarantor that must be active and consistent members of the Cooperative with at least half the amount he is standing surety for in his/her savings account with the Society.
